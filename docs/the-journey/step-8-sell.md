**Days 16-17 of your journey**

Your product is live. Now it needs users. Sell mode helps you create a landing page, tell your story, and get your first customers.

## Purpose

In Sell mode, 1M Code acts as a **growth marketer**. It will:

- Help you create a compelling landing page
- Craft your value proposition

- Guide you toward first revenue (if applicable)

## When to Use This Mode

Use Sell mode when:

- Your product is deployed and working
- You need a landing page
- You want to find your first users
- You're ready to tell the world

## The Landing Page

Your landing page needs to answer three questions in seconds:

### 1. What is it?
A clear headline that explains the product.

> "Never forget to drink water again."

### 2. Why should I care?
The benefit to the user.

> "Simple reminders that keep you hydrated during study sessions."

### 3. What do I do next?
A clear call to action.

> [Try it free] ‚Üí Links to your app

## Finding Your First Users

### Where to Post

| Platform | Best For | Tips |
|----------|----------|------|
| **Reddit** | Specific communities | Find your niche subreddit, provide value first |
| **Twitter/X** | Tech audience | Share your building journey, use relevant hashtags |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional network | Tell your story, celebrate shipping |
| **ProductHunt** | New products | Good for launch day visibility |
| **Indie Hackers** | Maker community | Share your journey and learnings |
| **Friends & Family** | First feedback | Ask them to try it and give honest feedback |

### The 10-User Challenge

Your goal: Get **10 real users** who aren't friends or family.

These 10 users will tell you:
- If your product actually solves a problem
- What's confusing or broken
- Whether they'd use it again

10 users is a small number, but it's harder than you think. If you can get 10 strangers to use your product, you've built something real.

## Collecting Feedback

### Simple Feedback Methods

1. **Google Form** ‚Äî Create a short survey
2. **Email link** ‚Äî "Email me at..."
3. **Twitter DMs** ‚Äî "DM me your thoughts"
4. **In-app feedback** ‚Äî Simple "Send Feedback" button

### Questions to Ask

- What's confusing about the app?
- What would make you use this every day?
- Would you recommend this to a friend?
- What features are missing?

## First Revenue (Optional)

If your product could charge money:

### Simple Payment Options
- **Stripe** ‚Äî For one-time or subscription payments
- **Gumroad** ‚Äî For simple digital product sales
- **Buy Me a Coffee** ‚Äî For tips/donations

### When to Charge
- After you've validated the product works
- When you have happy users asking for more features
- When you can offer a premium version

For MVP, **free is fine**. Get users first, monetize later.
